ChatGPT Companion Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

```

ChatGPT Companion is an unofficial browser extension that customizes the ChatGPT experience with companions, themes, sounds, avatars, and local visual settings.

What the extension stores

ChatGPT Companion stores your customization preferences locally in your browser so your settings can be remembered.

  • Selected companion and theme preferences
  • Sound and volume settings
  • Companion position and visual options
  • Uploaded background images and avatar choices

What we do not collect

ChatGPT Companion does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your ChatGPT conversations, prompts, responses, browsing history, or personal information with Dyadware.

Background images and avatars

Images you upload for backgrounds or avatars stay in your browser storage. They are not uploaded to Dyadware servers.

Support links

If you choose to support the developer through an external payment link, payments are handled by the external provider. ChatGPT Companion does not process or store payment information.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Dyadware through the project’s official support page or GitHub profile.

ChatGPT Companion is an unofficial extension and is not affiliated with OpenAI.
```
